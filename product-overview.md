# Uber Clean for Hospitals — Product Overview

## What it is
A B2B web app that gives hospital directors on-demand, medical-grade cleaning for surgical equipment, scrubs, and other required garments. Think Uber's dispatch model, applied to hospital linen and instrument reprocessing: request a pickup, track chain-of-custody, receive items back cleaned to regulatory standards.

## Target user
Hospital directors (and their operations/supply-chain delegates) at U.S. hospitals and surgical centers. They are accountable for infection control, OR turnover time, compliance (Joint Commission, AAMI ST79, OSHA, CDC), and vendor cost.

## Core value proposition
- **On-demand reliability** — replace fixed-schedule laundry/sterilization contracts with flexible pickups that match surgical volume.
- **Medical-grade guarantee** — every cycle meets sterilization and laundering standards with auditable documentation.
- **Single pane of glass** — one dashboard for scrubs, surgical instruments, and other garments instead of multiple vendors.
- **Compliance-ready** — automatic chain-of-custody logs, batch records, and audit reports.

## Primary jobs-to-be-done
1. Schedule a pickup for soiled instruments, scrubs, or garments.
2. Track items in real time through collection, cleaning/sterilization, and return.
3. Receive and verify compliance documentation for each cycle.
4. Manage billing, SLAs, and recurring service across multiple hospital departments.

## Form factor
Responsive web app (desktop-first for director/admin workflows; mobile-friendly for floor staff handling pickups and deliveries).

## Out of scope (v1)
- Consumer-grade laundry.
- Non-U.S. regulatory regimes.
- Native mobile apps.
- In-house sterilization equipment sales.

## Key success signals
- Turnaround time per pickup.
- % of cycles with complete compliance documentation.
- Director NPS and contract renewal rate.
- Cost per cleaned unit vs. incumbent vendors.
