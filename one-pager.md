# Uber Clean for Hospitals — Executive One-Pager

## The product
A B2B web app that delivers on-demand, medical-grade cleaning and sterilization for surgical instruments, scrubs, and hospital garments. Uber's dispatch model, applied to hospital reprocessing: request a pickup, track chain-of-custody, receive items back cleaned to regulatory standards — all from a single dashboard.

## Target user
**Hospital directors** at U.S. hospitals and surgical centers, plus their operations and supply-chain delegates.

They are accountable for:
- Infection control and patient safety
- OR turnover time and surgical throughput
- Regulatory compliance (Joint Commission, AAMI ST79, OSHA, CDC)
- Vendor cost and contract performance

## Main job-to-be-done
> *"Keep every OR stocked with clean, sterile, compliant instruments and garments — without babysitting vendors or risking an audit failure."*

Supporting JTBDs:
1. Schedule a pickup that matches real surgical volume, not a fixed contract schedule.
2. Track items in real time through collection, cleaning/sterilization, and return.
3. Produce audit-ready compliance documentation for every cycle.
4. Manage billing, SLAs, and recurring service across multiple departments in one place.

## Key pain points today
- **Rigid contracts** — fixed-schedule laundry and sterilization vendors don't flex with surgical volume, causing shortages on heavy days and waste on light ones.
- **Vendor sprawl** — scrubs, instruments, and garments are handled by different vendors with different systems, SLAs, and invoices.
- **Compliance anxiety** — chain-of-custody and batch records are often paper-based or fragmented, making Joint Commission and AAMI audits painful and risky.
- **No visibility** — directors can't tell where a tray or scrub set is once it leaves the floor, leading to OR delays and frantic phone calls.
- **Opaque cost** — hard to benchmark cost-per-cleaned-unit across vendors or justify spend to the CFO.

## Why it wins
- **On-demand reliability** replaces rigid contracts with flexible pickups.
- **Medical-grade guarantee** — every cycle meets sterilization and laundering standards with auditable documentation.
- **Single pane of glass** for scrubs, instruments, and garments.
- **Compliance-ready by default** — automated chain-of-custody logs, batch records, and audit reports.

## Success signals
- Turnaround time per pickup
- % of cycles with complete compliance documentation
- Director NPS and contract renewal rate
- Cost per cleaned unit vs. incumbent vendors
